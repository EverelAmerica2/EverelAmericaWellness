Our Work Procedure
It's easy to build your custom pool with us...
01
Consultation
The Myrtha Georgia team can provide consultation on new pool design, existing pool inspection or pool RenovAction – pool refurbishment.

Through commitment to engineering and innovation, Myrtha Pools has grown to be world leader among commercial pool builders. Embracing creative and architectural challenges, our hands-on approach and pursuit of excellence has resulted in an international portfolio that includes an enviable range of gold-medal competition venues, exciting community facilities, breathtaking hospitality pools and more. Our regular and custom swimming pools can be found in community centers, water parks, university campuses, cruise ships, medical centers, camping grounds and sports clubs all around the world.
02
Construction
The Myrtha Georgia team can work through pool installers, pool contractors or direct managed installations. In all cases Myrtha pools provide support and guidance to insure an optimum pool installation.

Installation Timing flexibility
The installation timing of a Myrtha pool is extremely flexible as the entire structure can be installed at whatever point in the construction schedule is best for the project and for the project management team. This allows the general contractor and the rest of the construction team to execute their total scope of work in the most convenient and safest environment.

Expedited Construction Schedule
Myrtha technology has shown that construction timelines for the swimming pool portion of a project is reduced in comparison to conventional concrete/tile pool construction methods. On average, Myrtha technology reduces the construction time for a 50m pool by 8 weeks and a 25m pool by 6 weeks. These time savings translate directly to project cost savings.
03
Hand Off
No matter who does the installation, the Myrtha Georgia team will perform a final inspection in order to certify the work and provide the 25 year warranty.
